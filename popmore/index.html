Popmore - Mystery Box Companion App
Reimagining Unboxing: A Digital Second Chance at Discovery
CONTEXT
Mystery boxes are rapidly gaining popularity, with Pop Mart at the center of the surprise-driven collecting culture.

As the trend grew, I began questioning what truly drives people to buy mystery boxes.
USER RESEARCH
To better understand the culture around mystery boxes, I visited Pop Mart's offline store and immersed myself in the experience firsthand. Alongside field research, I interviewed 9 participants to uncover key emotions, behaviors, and motivations.

I then synthesized the findings using the KJ method to uncover core themes.
Conducted a KJ Method using quotes and observation statements from the interviews. I organized them into categories, grouped similar ideas, and took time to analyze emerging patterns and themes.
KEY INSIGHT
I found that for many users, the true value of a mystery box lies in the excitement of unboxing rather than the item inside.
DESIGN SOLUTION
What if unboxing could happen twice: once in your hands, and again on your screen?
When a customer buys a physical mystery box, the app also gives them a second digital box to open.
CORE FEATURE 1
Personalized Collection
Users can view, showcase, and organize their figures in a digital collection space, arranging them however they like.
CORE FEATURE 2
Claim a Digital Figure
When a user purchases a physical mystery box, they can unlock a matching digital figure in the app, giving them a second unboxing experience to relive.
CORE FEATURE 3
Home Screen Widget
Figures can be added directly to the device's home screen as interactive widgets.
Bringing the collection beyond the app and into daily life.
REFLECTION
Conducting in-depth research on potential users highlighted the importance of understanding not only what the audience wants, but also the reasoning behind their decisions.
